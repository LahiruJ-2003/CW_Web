TEAMS

We design. We manufacture. We race. Our team has set a new benchmark for success, winning consecutive Drivers’ and Constructors’ World Championships in 2014, 2015, 2016, 2017, 2018, 2019 and 2020, and the Constructors’ Championship in 2021. During these title-winning seasons, the team achieved 111 wins, 232 podium finishes, 118 pole positions, 81 fastest laps and 53 one-two finishes from 160 race starts.

Technical Team

Engineering, technology and performance combine to drive racing success. Lightning-fast production. High-impact projects. A stimulating environment for any technical mind. Three distinct areas form our world-class technical team: Engineering, Technology, and Performance. Together, we focus on innovation, reliability, and flawless execution. We work tirelessly to deliver a car that can achieve the best performance – maximising the opportunity to score world championship points, one race after another. We aim to outperform our competitors with the very best technology and an unrivalled development rate to achieve championship success. Our technology capability strategy drives investment in long-term sustainable performance. We continuously develop class-leading tools and processes, enhancing our ability to understand vehicle performance factors and produce optimal solutions and designs.

Operations Team

From Brackley to the Grand Prix circuits of the world. High-precision processes. Highly skilled people. High performance throughout. In a sport where speed is just as important as precision, the operational challenges are about as rigorous as they come. Steely nerves and quick thinking are essential. Our Operations teams use the latest materials, the highest precision processes and employ the most highly skilled people in the industry to ensure quality is at the heart of our race programme. We work to tight timescales to get the car built and race ready as early as possible. We invest in the very latest machinery, enabling production to deliver the very best output, both in terms of precision and finish. In a world where both speed and meticulous attention to detail are paramount, we are constantly reviewing and refining what we do to deliver a better output, faster.

Race Team

Trackside support for a car with the biggest ambitions in Formula One. Pin-sharp analysis. Instant feedback. An ever-changing environment. Wherever we are in the world, our Race team supports our cars on the track, giving our drivers the edge with deft analysis and quick feedback. The Race team is a close-knit group of people travelling to races, all over the globe. Our focus is to win championships in the shortest time possible. We calculate the strategy in an ever-changing environment, refine the set-up of the car and ensure the drivers have the best car beneath them as they leave the garage. In a sport where every fraction of a second counts, our job is to eliminate errors and missed opportunities to add performance. We believe in a culture of positive, constructive challenge and continuous improvement, focusing on quality, reliability, and speed.

Business Team

The teams that empower a world-class sporting operation. Showcasing our brand. Building relationships. Operating at our best. We couldn’t win races without amazing IT, sharp Legal minds, skilled Marketing experts, shrewd Finance and visionary HR. Each of these disciplines brings their expertise to an environment where everything was needed yesterday. Our Business Support functions provide the infrastructure that our team needs in the championship campaign. Our Marketing experts are responsible for securing and building the best relationships with our partners and showcasing the best of our brand. Legal provides the sound contractual foundations upon which our relationships are built. IT enables the huge computing capability of our team and Finance makes sure the numbers speak for themselves and that we operate compliantly. Finally, our HR division continually works to build the capability of our workforce and the development of our inclusive culture.
